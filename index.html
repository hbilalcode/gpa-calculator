📝 How GPA is Calculated:

Subject Grade Points: Based on percentage obtained

Semester GPA = (Σ(Grade Points × Credit Hours)) / Σ(Credit Hours)

Overall CGPA = (Σ(Semester GPA × Credit Hours)) / Σ(Credit Hours)
